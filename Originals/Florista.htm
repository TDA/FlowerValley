Florista is a chain of floral designing boutiques across India, It is the retail arm of Karuturi Global Limited, the world’s largest grower of Roses.

Karuturi is among the world’s top 25 agro – transnational corporations with presence in India, Ethiopia, Kenya, Dubai and Holland. It is a listed entity with a market cap of over 1200 Crores. Karuturi’s business interests include Agriculture, Floriculture, Food Processing and Internet services.

Florista’s specialty is in designing exquisite flower arrangements that are made from exotic, fresh and beautiful flowers, sourced from all parts of the world including India.

Our team consists of floral design experts as well as artists who customize bouquets and floral arrangements using a

  

combination of varied flowers, vases and accessories to go along with the arrangement.

Our specialty lies in the fact that our flowers are hand delivered by our well-uniformed representatives in the cities of our existence.

Just call (+91 22 43449000) in (Mumbai) to place an order for our floral arrangements.

Buying from us
For those of you who wish to avail the beauty of our flowers, besides Mumbai, you can log on to our website www.florista.in via which you can purchase the arrangements.

We deliver flowers across the World
Visit our secure and easy to use website (www.florista.in) to send flowers to anywhere in India and worldwide, with complete

  

confidence that your flowers will be delivered to your total satisfaction.

You have the convenience of ordering flowers 24x7 via our website and you can rest assured that your expectations will be met with utmost quality, value and professional service.

We have an Offline Customer Care Centre (91 22 43449000) which is open Monday through Saturday from 8.00 Am to 8.00 Pm and on Sundays from 8.00 Am to 6.00 Pm IST, to help you with all your queries.
